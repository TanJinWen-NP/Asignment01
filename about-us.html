About Us

What Is Terraria?

Our Journey

The Earthy Story Behind Terraria

Terraria was founded in 1998 by Elliza Beloquez - currently the CEO. She's been fascinated with terrariums at a young age and have been constructing her very own since the age of 9. Once she got older, she took a leap of faith and built an earthy empire aimed to bring nature to the desk of those who shared similar interest. Since then, the company has been blooming and rising through the markets rapidly. We strive to continue our work here and provide terrariums until the very end!

Our Mission

Cheap And Aesthetic Terrariums

Our brand envisions a team that prioritises quality. We ensure terrariums are constructed for different needs be it decoration or not. We have the best suppliers and team working diligently to provide aesthetic and renewable terrariums that do not break your bank.

Our Greatness

We're Not Your Average Brand

Unlike most retailers, Terraria allows you to customise your personal terrariums. Choose your own decorations, materials and resources such as wood, plants and tiny-life! You are our first priority. Our brand is confident in its abililty to provide unique and personal experiences and terrariums for our customers.
