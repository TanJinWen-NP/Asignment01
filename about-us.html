Our Story

About Us

Terraria was founded in 1987, by Elisha Mendaz - CEO. Upon graduation from Stanford University with a PHD in Ecology, she started Terraria at the age of 33. Until now, we've aspired to provide our customers with the best terraria's at the highest quality at affordable prices. Elisha Mandez made it a promise to keep true to the freedom of customisation. Thats why we're leading the market in terrariums, with a wide array of services and top-notch terrarias. Join us, on our way to the top and this revolutionary journey.
