Dark Hallons

Featured Plants: Monsterra, Venus Guy Traps, Moss & Sea Moss.

Description

Dark Hallons is inspired by the nature architect Denzel Molen. With phosphorescent corals, soils and plants hailing from New Jersey and South New Delhi, it's one of the most sought after terrariums in any realm of human endeavour. Venus Guy Traps from Mexico add that extra kick to it bringing it closer to perfection. Easy maintenance, 26 inches and 14 inches for its dimensions, it's on road to being one of the most prized possesions anyone may have.
